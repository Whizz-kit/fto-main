Who We Are

Find The Others (FTO) is a curated directory and community platform dedicated to connecting individuals with consciousness-expanding practices, spaces, and practitioners. We serve as a bridge for those seeking to deepen their understanding of themselves and the world through various modalities such as meditation, breathwork, plant medicine, and communal living.

What We Offer

  • Directory: A searchable database of aligned communities, retreat centers, and individual practitioners.
  • Events: A listing of workshops, ceremonies, and gatherings globally.
  • News & Insights: Articles and research on consciousness topics.
  • Community: A Discord-based community for real-time connection and support.

Our Values

  • Consciousness Expansion: Supporting safe and authentic exploration of altered states and mindfulness.
  • Grounded Practice: Emphasizing integration and practical application of spiritual insights.
  • Authentic Connection: Fostering genuine relationships based on shared values.
  • Reverence: Respecting all beings and the planet.

Pricing & Membership

Access to the directory and basic community features is currently free. We offer paid listings for practitioners and centers who wish to be featured more prominently. Future plans include a membership tier for exclusive content and events.

FAQ

How can I join the directory?
You can submit your listing through the "Submit Listing" button on our homepage. All submissions are reviewed for alignment with our values.
Is FTO only for plant medicine?
No. While we include plant medicine centers, we embrace a wide range of consciousness practices including meditation, yoga, breathwork, and eco-villages.
